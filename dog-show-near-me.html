Know your options
Types of dog shows
There are many ways to show off your dog. Here's the landscape, from formal to ridiculous (that's us).
🏆
AKC Conformation Shows
The classic. Registered purebreds are judged against breed standards by licensed judges. Requires training, a handler, and breed registration. Entry fees typically $30–$50 per class.
🏃
Agility & Rally Events
Dogs navigate obstacle courses against the clock. Open to mixed breeds through AKC Canine Partners. Requires training and practice. Great fun if your dog listens to you (ours doesn't).
🌇
Local Fun Shows
Community events at parks and fairs with categories like "waggiest tail" and "best trick." Usually inexpensive, open to all breeds, and much more relaxed than formal shows. Check local event listings.
💻
Online Dog Shows
Photo or video-based contests you enter from home. Some are voting galleries with paid votes (we're looking at you, PupVote). The Dog Show is a live, real-time show with no pay-to-win mechanics.
