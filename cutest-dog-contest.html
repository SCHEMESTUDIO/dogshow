Why every dog wins
A cutest dog contest without losing dogs

⚖
"Cutest" isn't measurable
Cuteness is a feeling, not a metric. Two people can look at the same dog and one sees a perfect angel, the other sees a creature. So there's no judge — just an audience cheering for the dogs they love.
👑
A crown, but no losers
The most-cheered dog each month is crowned Best in Show. Fans cheer with bones, and you can buy more bones to rally support — so the crown is partly pay-to-support. But it's a permanent honor, not a cash prize, and no dog is ever eliminated.
🐶
No dog deserves to lose
If you've ever entered a dog in a cutest dog contest and watched them come in 7th, you know it feels weird. Here, every dog keeps its page forever and gets its own moment on stage — crown or no crown.
