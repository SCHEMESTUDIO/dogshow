About The Dog Show

The Dog Show is a live, online dog-viewing experience where viewers watch dogs appear one at a time in a shared, real-time slideshow. Premium members can upload their own dogs to appear in the show, earning bones (votes) from the community. Each dog that appears gets a permanent certificate page like this one, documenting their moment in the spotlight.

Whether you're looking for a live dog show online, a dog photo contest, or simply a place to celebrate good dogs, The Dog Show has you covered. Join hundreds of viewers watching dogs live at dogshow.lol.

Dog Shows, Dog Contests & Dog Photo Competitions

Unlike traditional dog shows that require in-person attendance and breed standards, The Dog Show celebrates every dog — mixed breeds, rescues, puppies, and seniors alike. Our online dog show format means anyone can participate from anywhere in the world. Premium members get their dog featured in the live show, complete with a shareable certificate and stats page.

Looking for a cute dog contest online? The Dog Show's bone voting system lets viewers react to each dog in real time. The most loved dogs earn the most bones, creating a fun and friendly competition.

Submit Your Dog

Want your dog to appear on The Dog Show? Get a premium membership ($3.99) and upload your dog's photo. Our AI verifies it's a real dog, and then your pup enters the rotation — appearing to all live viewers with your name credited. Every appearance earns stats, bones, and a permanent certificate page you can share with friends and family.
