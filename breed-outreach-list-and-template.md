# Breed Outreach — Prospect List + Email Template

**Goal:** build relationships + backlinks for dogshow.lol's `/breeds/{slug}` pages.
**The deal (per James):** invite them to contribute a *quote/tip* for our breed page → we credit them with a **link back to their site** (the carrot, benefits them). Then softly ask if **we can write a piece for their blog** → **link back to dogshow.lol** (the prize, benefits us).
**Compiled:** 2026-06-23. Prospects found via web search + page fetch. Two entries (Lost Creek, Mini Golden Paws) independently re-verified by me; the rest are at the confidence noted.

---

## How to use this (read first — 2 min)

1. **Personalize the `[detail]` line every time.** One sentence referencing their actual site/recent post. Generic blasts get ignored and can ding your sending reputation. Budget ~1 min per email.
2. **Send from a real, monitored address** (`james@wearescheme.studio` or a `@dogshow.lol` address) — never no-reply.
3. **Lead with the carrot.** The quote ask (a link *to them*) is the easy yes. Keep the guest-post ask (a link *to you*) as the secondary line so you're not opening with the bigger lift.
4. **Contact method matters.** Where there's a verified email, you can send the full email. Where it's a **contact form / IG DM**, use the short variant below — those are flagged "manual" in the list.
5. **Keep it editorial — don't pay for links.** A paid link exchange violates Google's guidelines and risks both sites.

### Honest expectations (so you calibrate effort)
- **These are mostly small breeder/owner sites.** Their links are *topically relevant* (good) but *low domain authority* (modest individual weight). The play is **volume of relevant links**, not a few big ones. *(Confidence: Medium-High.)*
- **Some breeders may hesitate to link out** to anything that smells like competition for attention. Frame dogshow.lol as *complementary* — a fun, free place to show off the dogs they've bred/raised, not a rival. *(Medium.)*
- **Expect some `rel="nofollow"` links.** Still worth it: referral traffic + topical relevance. *(High.)*
- **Realistic reply rate on cold outreach like this is ~10–25%** even when personalized. Send to all ~30 to land a handful. *(Medium — depends heavily on personalization.)*
